"Full Creative" gives you a unique training program called "Internship". It is a policy of Full Creative that every fresher who enters into the company should have to complete this program. Under this training program every Software developers/Testers/Designers will undergo different types of technologies and will learn many new programming languages. In this Internship, every developer will get one mentor for assistance and guiding. They will be giving some tasks to do for interns.

Full Creative tells how world works. Full Creative is a place where it gives freedom for interns to explore themselves and to learn new things in the present world. Internship is a 3 months program where for every month end reviews have been done to know status of their learning. For every month their will be new interns coming in.
